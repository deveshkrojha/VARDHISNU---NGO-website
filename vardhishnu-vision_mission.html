About Us

Vardhishnu – is a not-for-profit organisation working to create spaces of learning and support to street children specially child waste picker and child labors..'If we want to real peace in the world, we should start educating our children', inspired by this quote of Mahatma Gandhi, Vardhishnu a not-for-profit organisaiton was initiated Our aim to work on specific child issues through empirical research & implementation based on its findings. by a group of young individuals.



Vision :-Every child will have a safe, secure and happy childhood

Mission :-To create an eco-system of learning and support for children by partnering with individuals, institutions and iniative
